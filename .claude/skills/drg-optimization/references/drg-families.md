## Key Insight: DRG Family Shifts

Some documentation improvements don't just upgrade within a
family — they shift the case to an entirely different (higher-
weighted) DRG family:

- HTN + HF documented separately -> DRG 304-305 (Hypertension)
- HTN + HF linked as hypertensive heart disease -> DRG 291-293 (HF & Shock)
- Revenue impact of family shift: additional $4,000-$7,000
