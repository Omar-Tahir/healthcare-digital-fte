# Payer-Specific Rules — Reference

## Medicare

- **Filing deadline:** 365 days from date of service
- **Appeal deadline:** 120 days from initial determination
- **PA requirements:** Limited for most Part A services;
  required for some DME and Part B drugs
- **LCD/NCD:** Check MAC jurisdiction for local coverage
- **Two-midnight rule:** Inpatient admission requires expected
  LOS >= 2 midnights; otherwise observation status
- **HAC penalties:** 14 categories of hospital-acquired
  conditions with payment reduction
- **Sequestration:** Automatic 2% reduction on all claims

## Medicaid

- **Filing deadline:** Varies by state (90 days to 3 years)
- **Shortest deadline:** New York = 90 days
- **PA requirements:** Vary significantly by state
- **Managed Medicaid:** Different rules per MCO within state
- **Dual-eligible patients:** Medicare primary, Medicaid
  secondary — different rules for each

## UnitedHealthcare

- **Filing deadline:** 90-180 days depending on plan
- **Appeal deadline:** 180 days
- **PA requirements:** Extensive list, updated quarterly
- **Bundling rules:** More aggressive than NCCI edits
- **Clinical review:** Optum clinical guidelines (proprietary)

## Aetna

- **Filing deadline:** 90-180 days depending on plan
- **Appeal deadline:** 180 days
- **PA requirements:** Clinical Policy Bulletins (CPBs)
- **Step therapy:** Required for many specialty drugs

## Cigna

- **Filing deadline:** 90-365 days depending on plan
- **Appeal deadline:** 365 days
- **PA requirements:** Coverage policies updated monthly
- **eviCore:** Uses eviCore for specialty PA reviews

## Anthem/BCBS

- **Filing deadline:** 90-180 days (varies by state plan)
- **Appeal deadline:** 180 days
- **Note:** Each state BCBS plan has different rules
- **AIM Specialty Health:** Uses AIM for imaging PA

## Humana

- **Filing deadline:** 180 days
- **Appeal deadline:** 180 days
- **MA plans:** Medicare Advantage rules may differ from
  commercial Humana plans

## Tricare

- **Filing deadline:** 365 days
- **Appeal deadline:** 90 days (shortest appeal window)
- **Rules:** Generally follow Medicare coverage policies
