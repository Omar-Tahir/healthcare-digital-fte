## Exemptions

- **Emergency procedures:** Generally exempt from PA
- **Inpatient orders:** Exempt at most payers (PA is for
  the admission itself, not individual inpatient orders)
- **Cancer staging follow-up:** Often exempt
- **Urgent/emergent imaging:** Typically exempt but may
  require retrospective notification within 48-72 hours

## Key Statistics

- 86% of PA denials are overturned on appeal (AMA 2023)
- This indicates initial denials are often documentation
  gaps, not clinical inappropriateness
- CDI agent can help by ensuring required documentation
  exists BEFORE the PA request is submitted
